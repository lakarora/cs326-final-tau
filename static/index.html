Passage

Passage is a book marketplace that allows students of the Five College Consortium to buy and sell books to one another. Search for books for your classes, or even just for leisure. Since we compare prices to books on Amazon you can guarentee that you are getting the most out of every book exchange.



    Supported Schools:
  • Amherst College
  • Hampshire College
  • Mount Holyoke College
  • Smith College
  • University of Massachusetts Amherst
